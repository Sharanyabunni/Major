How It Works

Our system uses a sophisticated process to analyze carotid ultrasound images and predict coronary artery disease.

1

Image Acquisition

Focused carotid ultrasound images are captured using standard ultrasound equipment and uploaded to our system.

2

Feature Extraction

Our system extracts color histograms, Hu moments, Haralick texture features, and color texture patterns from the ultrasound images.

3

Ensemble Analysis

Multiple machine learning algorithms including Random Forest, KNN, XGBoost, and Naive Bayes analyze the extracted features.

4

Prediction

The ensemble model combines individual predictions to provide a final assessment with confidence scores.
